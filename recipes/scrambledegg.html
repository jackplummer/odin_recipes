Scrambled Egg

This is a recipe for scrambled egg. The image is free-to-use and has been reproduced from: Unsplash.com

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 6 tbsp milk
  • Butter

Instructions

  1. Whisk eggs and milk. Add a pinch of salt.
  2. Heat a small frying pan and add a small knob of butter.
  3. Pour in the egg mixutre and let it sit for 20 seconds.
  4. Fold the eggs until you have the desired consistency.
  5. Leave to cook until desired.
  6. Serve with toast and season with pepper.
