Spaghetti Carbonara

This is a recipe for a great Spaghetti Carbonara. The image is free-to-use and has been re-produced from: Unsplash.com.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • 2 Eggs
  • 3 Cloves of Garlic
  • 2 Slices of Bacon
  • Mushrooms
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Parsley (Optional)
  • Salt and Pepper

Instructions

  1. Cook spaghetti.
  2. Cut up the bacon, garlic, and mushrooms. Then fry on a low heat.
  3. Beat two eggs with a small amount of parmesan and season with pepper and parsley.
  4. Once spaghetti is cooked, drain and leave a small amount of pasta water.
  5. Take bacon mix off of heat and add spaghetti. Mix well.
  6. Add the egg mix and mix well. Then add more parmesan and mix again.
  7. Season and then serve with garnish of parsley.
