Egg-Fried Rice

This is a recipe for egg-fried rice. The image is free-to-use and has been reproduced from: Unsplash.com

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Vegetable Oil
  • 1 Onion
  • 4 eggs
  • Soy sauce

Instructions

  1. Cook rice, drain and set aside.
  2. Using oil, fry finely chopped onions until browned.
  3. Add rice and stir for a couple of minutes.
  4. Create hole in middle of rice and add in the egg mixture, leave to set for a few seconds and stir into rice.
  5. Season with soy sauce to taste.
